About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my portfolio! My name is Francisco Ruiz, but all of my friends call me Frank. I am the third of four siblings(two sisters and a younger brother). Like many, my parents migrated to the Untied States at a young age in order to make a better life for what would soon become a family of six. Thanks to them, the characteristics, and values they instilled in me; I have been able to become part of the first generation in my family to earn a college education.

I am enrolled in this class because I think its a great opportunity. I started coding in April of 2015 and have a desire to learn as much of it as possible. I would like to land a job as a jr. developer, preferably on the front end. I would like this role because I feel programming would keep me more engaged in my work then things I have done in the past. I would like to always be learing new things and growing professionally. As Napoleon Hill put it:

If your not learning while your earning, your cheating yourself out of the better portion of your compensation.
